Our Story

About
A-Town Hoops.

Basketball for
Everyone.

A-Town Hoops is a not-for-profit basketball organization in Arlington, Washington. We provide quality instruction and believe the game should be accessible to every kid who wants to play.

We supported over 150 athletes last season through our AAU and SWISH programs — and we're growing every year.

Whether you're picking up a basketball for the first time or you've been playing for years, A-Town is a place where you can develop your skills, make friends, and fall in love with the game.

Our Mission
"Basketball should be accessible to everyone in Arlington."
We build an environment where any kid — experienced or brand new — can learn the game, have fun, and build relationships that carry beyond the court. All coaches and board members give their time as volunteers.
